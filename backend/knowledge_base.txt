## Common Issues & FAQs

Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
A: Yes, plan changes take effect immediately. Upgrades are prorated; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

Q: What happens when I exceed my automation run limit?
A: We'll notify you at 80% usage. Runs above the limit are paused until the next billing cycle, or you can upgrade your plan.

Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes, you can export all your data as CSV or JSON at any time from the Settings page.

Q: Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or startups?
A: Yes, we offer 50% off for verified nonprofits and early-stage startups. Contact sales@company.com to apply.

Q: Is there an API?
A: Yes, all Pro and Enterprise plans include full API access with detailed documentation at docs.company.com/api.
